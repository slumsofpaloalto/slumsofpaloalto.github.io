How to succeed
through relentless failure

Silicon Valley, the real story

The story of a family that has weathered a series of ten startups and the environment that is the real “Shark Tank”: Silicon Valley. A place where some people are very badly off, others struggle to keep afloat, but the news continuously reports on the minority of winners, even glossing over these winners’ dark sides and failures.

  • For the price of a Palo Alto mocha, buy the Kindle, English, Full Color version

An insider view on a series of ten startups, with no unicorns, and the lessons learned from their similarities and differences.

  • For the price of a Mountain View appetizer, buy the Printed, English, B&W version

Two daughters raised and educated within the tank by leveraging communities within Palo Alto that most people have never heard of.

  • For the price of a Los Altos meal, buy the Printed, English, Full Color version

Relentless failing at work and in life, relentless financial struggle, and how to progress through it all… because there is no other choice. Family, community, and commitment come first.
