Synopsis

The story of a family that is weathering a series of Silicon Valley startups and the environment that is the real “Shark Tank”. A tank where some people are very badly off, others struggle to keep afloat, but the news continuously reports on the minority of winners.

A series of ten startups, with no unicorns, and the lessons learned from them.

Two daughters raised within the Shark Tank by leveraging communities within Palo Alto that most people have never heard of.

Relentless failing at work and in life, relentless financial struggle, and the progress through it all because there is no other choice.
